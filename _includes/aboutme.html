About Me

I just join Tianjin University as a lectuer at the School of Computer Science and Technology. Previously, I received the B.S. degree and M.Sc. degree from Heilongjiang University in 2011 and 2014, and the Ph.D. degree from Dalian University of Technology under the supervision of Prof.Peihua Li in 2018. My research is on computer vision and pattern recognition, particularly visual classification and deep probability distribution modeling.
